# Liquidity

Liquidity providers (LPs) deposit token pairs into Addax pools and earn swap fees from trades that pass through their active price range.

Unlike traditional AMMs, Addax uses concentrated liquidity — you choose the price range where your capital sits. When the market price is inside your range, you earn fees. When it moves outside, you stop earning and hold a larger proportion of the less valuable token until price returns.
