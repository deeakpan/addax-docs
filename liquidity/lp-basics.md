# LP Basics

Liquidity providers deposit token pairs to earn fees from swaps.

## In concentrated liquidity

- Capital is placed in chosen price ranges, not across all prices.
- Active range earns fees; out-of-range does not.
- Position behavior depends on price movement relative to chosen bounds.

## LP tradeoffs

- Narrow range: higher fee efficiency, higher rebalance risk.
- Wide range: lower maintenance, lower fee concentration.
