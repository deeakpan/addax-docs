# Fees

Fees can exist at multiple layers:

- Pool swap fee tier (for LPs)
- Protocol-level fee (if configured)
- Execution/filler economics (offchain market)

## What users should understand

- Higher fee tiers may still be better if they offer deeper liquidity.
- Effective execution cost = pool fee + price impact + route complexity.
- Best route selection should optimize net output, not just nominal fee tier.
